How does prepayment work?

When you make a lump-sum payment over your regular EMI, the entire amount reduces your outstanding principal immediately. Since interest is charged on the remaining principal every month, a lower principal means less interest — starting from the very next EMI.

↓
Principal drops instantly
The full prepayment amount reduces your loan balance right away, not spread over future EMIs.
⇉
Two modes
Reduce Tenure: Close the loan early, keep the same EMI. Reduce EMI: Lower your monthly payment, keep the same end date.
⏰
Earlier saves more
Prepaying in year 1 saves significantly more interest than the same amount paid in year 5, because it eliminates more compounding cycles.
